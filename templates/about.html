CIS422 Project #2: BookBucket


Motivation

This application is meant to be a central resource so that any student or teacher can access the books that are required for any given class. In the case of a teacher they would also be able to add or remove classes they are teaching in a given term and add or remove books for individual classes they are teaching. The hope is that users will also be given a resource telling them where to find books in the case that the school does not have any virtual copies.

Tenets

  • Allow Students to read their textbooks online and/or find resources to locate and rent or purchase the book elsewhere.
  • Allow Teachers to read the instructor copy of any book they require as well as add or remove books from classes that they are teaching.
  • Allow Admins the ability to add or remove students/teachers/classes/books to their tables on the database if they are needed to be at last minute.
  • Allow anyone the ability to see the list of books that are available and even refine it down to those of classes they have taken in the past.
  • Allow anyone with access to one of the virtual books to take snapshots of page resources but not allow them to download the whole pdf.
